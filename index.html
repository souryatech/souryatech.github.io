Machine Learning for Medicine

I'm passionate about applying machine learning to medical imaging and healthcare challenges. My work focuses on developing intelligent systems for medical image analysis, particularly in abnormality and tumor detection.

My research explores text-guided object detection architectures for identifying abnormalities in chest X-rays, combining computer vision with natural language processing to enhance diagnostic capabilities. I'm driven by the potential of AI to assist healthcare professionals and improve patient outcomes.
